Namespace Consistency (4)

  • �Collection resources MAY list the URLs of non-WebDAV compliant children in the HTTP URL namespace hierarchy as internal members but are not required to do so.�

    • For example, if the resource with URL http://foo.com/bar/blah is not WebDAV compliant
    • and the URL http://foo.com/bar/ identifies a collection
    • then URL http://foo.com/bar/blah may or may not be an internal member of the collection with URL http://foo.com/bar/.
