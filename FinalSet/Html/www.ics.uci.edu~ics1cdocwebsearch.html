Searching the Web

New Spiders Roam the Web, by John December, offers a fine overview of the subject.

Here are some ways of searching the Web:

  • World-Wide Web Virtual Library: Subject Catalogue from the W3 Consortium.
  • EINet Galaxy from EINet, a commercial concern.
  • Lycos from Carnegie-Mellon University.
  • Harvest from University of Colorado.
  • WWW Worm from University of Colorado.
  • WebCrawler from University of Washington.
