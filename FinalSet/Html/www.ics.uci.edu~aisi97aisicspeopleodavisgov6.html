Confederate Tribe of Net Natives Economy

The Confederate Tribe of Net Natives economy is based on casino's, Indian jewelry and other small businesses. We have an advanced medical center  that keeps up with current technology and innovations. On the Inner Tribal Reservation all schools are under the control of the Net Natives School System. Native Planet  is the sole Internet Service Provider for the Tribe. The Inner Tribal Reservation population of then thousand is a diverse group comprised of many representatives of different tribes, that get along with each other like brothers and sisters.
