Person: Herman Welts

Birthday: 10 April 1857
Birthplace: Phillips, Maine
Mother: Emily Jane Allen
Father: Amos Welts
Sex: male

  • Married to Fanny M. Fox, 8 April 1876 at Lawrence, Massachusetts
  • Grace Ethel Welts born 24 September 1888 at Dracut, Massachusetts

The birthplace (Phillips) is from his great-granddaughter Anne Louise MacLaughlin's application to join the Society of Mayflower Descendants, which states that the information came from a marriage certificate. However Grace Ethel Welts' birth certificate states that her father's birthplace was instead Bath, Maine.
