The Judah Langsam Family


Judah Langsam was married to Hanie Ecker. This information comes from census, naturalization and NY marriage records, as researched by Ms. Suzan Wynne. Judah and Hanie had one known son.

  • Edward - Yitzchok in Hebrew (April 03, 1870 - June 11, 1932) was married twice. His first wife, Katie, bore him two children before passing away at the age of 31 on June 16, 1901. Edward remarried a widow, Rosie Cohen Eichner, that same year. He had 2 additional children with her. Edward naturalized in 1892, while living at 57 Lewis Street, in NYC. He later moved to 319 8th Street in NYC. In his later years he resided in Boston with his wife and children.
