I am a PhD student of Donald Bren School of Information and Computer Science, University of California, Irvine from 09/2011 to 09/2013.

 

My research interests lie in Human Computer Interaction and Ubiquitous Computing. Specifically, improving user experience by exploring Novel Interaction Techniques.

 

I earned my Master of Science degree in Computer Science, Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University, Beijing, China in June, 2011. My advisor is Prof. Yuanchun SHI. At Tsinghua, I worked on several projects, but the major one is exploring 3D interaction possibilities of mobile device by using on-board camera and motion sensors.
