Mid-size sportfisher

Our Sportfisher is a 32 foot cruiser, known as the "Hornsby". The "Hornsby" features state-of-the-art fish finding equipment, a full galley, bathrooms, flying bridge, and a tuna tower.

Hornsby also features two fighting chairs for big game fish, and a live bait capture system. There are two 200 gallon bait tanks, and two catch storage bins.
