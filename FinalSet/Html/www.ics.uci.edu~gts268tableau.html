Vigenère



The leftmost column has the keys; the top alphabet has the plaintext. So:

message HELLOWORLD

key     ECSECSECSE

cipher  LGDPQOSTHD






Beaufort



Find the plaintext in the left alphabet, and trace in until you find
the key letter. Then turn a right angle and go up. The letter at the
top alphabet is the ciphertext.

message HELLOWORLD

key     ECSECSECSE

cipher  WYHSOWPLHB






Variant Beaufort



Find the key letter in the left alphabet, and trace in until you find
the plaintext  letter. Then turn a right angle and go up. The letter at
the top alphabet is the ciphertext.

message HELLOWORLD

key     ECSECSECSE

cipher  DCTHMEKPTZ
