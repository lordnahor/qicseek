The Pesach Langsam Family


Pesach Langsam was married to Bessie Schechtor. This information comes from census, birth and death records, as researched by Ms. Estelle Guzik and Ms. Suzan Wynne. Pesach and Bessie had one known son.

  • Morris (1861 - Jun 4, 1912 [Sivan 20]) was married to Lena/Sarah Meth and had seven known children. He probably arrived in 1884 at the age of 23, according to ship records. He was layed to rest in Washington Cemetery (cemetery 5, Post 518, section #3, row 2, grave 4 - Ind. Order Brith Abraham) in Brooklyn, NY. His death record gives his place of death as Jewish Hospital. His tombstone indicates that his family comes from Dynow, Poland.
