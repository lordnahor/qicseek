I am currently conducting research on adult women joining technical learning organizations and informal learning groups such as Railsbridge, PyLadies, DIYGirls, and Hackbright Academy.  Many women seek exposure, during their post-graduate years, to disciplines like computer science and engineering, and use these informal learning channels to facilitate development of skills, if not a complete career pivot, into these technical disciplines.

The goal of this research is to understand, explore, and compare the novel ways these organizations and groups facilitate a new ecology of technical learning that stimulates and supports women's interest in these scientific fields.
