Private Charters

Our private charters take place on our sportfishing boat, the Hornsby. Reservations for one-day private charters must be made at least two weeks in advance, and two-day trip reservations must be a month in advance. Private charters are held only on weekends and are for parties ranging in size from one to eight people. These trips can be very customized according to the party leader. Since they are paying by the hour, they may request a spot, or ask for the captain's advice on the best spot for certain fish. Charges will be based upon length of trip, number of members in the party, and conditions for fishing.

To assure our clients of a good fishing experience, we have provided an exclusive insurance policy, for information click here.
