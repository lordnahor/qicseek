Activating a Resource for Content Negotiation (New)

  • Activating a resource for content negotiation.

    • Since most resources do not support negotiation, there must be some way to turn this capability on (and off).
    • Alternately, this could be formulated as having resources which always perform (or do not perform) content negotiation, and hence a means must be provided to create resources which always performs content negotiation.
