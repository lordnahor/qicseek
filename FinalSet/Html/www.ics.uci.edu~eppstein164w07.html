ICS 164, Winter 2007:
Computational Geometry

General Course Information

Coursework will consist of weekly homeworks, a midterm, and a comprehensive final exam. The course text will be Computational Geometry Algorithms and Applications, 2nd ed., by de Berg, van Kreveld, Overmars, and Schwarzkopf (Springer-Verlag, 2000). The course meets Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays, 2:00 - 2:50 in ICF 101.

Homeworks will be assigned on Fridays, due in the ICS distribution center the following Friday.
