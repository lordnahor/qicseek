The Necha Adler nee Langsam Family


Necha Langsam daughter of Israel Langsam and Peya Lemmer of Sienawa, Poland, was married to Jacob Adler, son of Aron and Feiga/Fentiny of Manasterez, Poland. This information comes from Polish records, as researched by Mr. Arye Barkai and Ms. Suzan Wynne. Necha and Isaac married in 1926 and had one known son.

  • Eliezer (1931 - 1933) was only two years old when he died in the town of Manasterez.
