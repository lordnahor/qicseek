  1. What are the most popular spices for the various cuisines in Hungary? The two most popular spices in Hungary are paprika and garlic. Strings of ungrinded red pepper are hung to dry and then crushed. These crushed red peppers are what is commonly referred to as paprika. Paprika is a key ingredient to Goulash, which is one of the most famous Hungarian dishes. Although goulash was originally a soup, it has turned into a hearty stew. Now, people commonly add plenty of meat, carrots, and potatoes to goulash. Goulash is just one of the many delicious cuisines from Hungary.
