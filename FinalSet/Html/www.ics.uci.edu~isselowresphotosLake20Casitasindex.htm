Lake Casitas Trip

Feb 2002

Lake Casitas is a beautiful water reservoir located at an elevation of 1,500 feet. It has 2,500 surface acres. Casitas has over 30 miles of fishable shoreline and nearly 500 campsites. The lake also has camping, hiking, and a water park.
