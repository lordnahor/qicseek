Khanh Nguyen


I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science Department at UCI. My advisor is Prof. Guoqing (Harry) Xu .
My research focuses on static and dynamic program analysis. Currently I'm involved in developing a transforming technique that can be scalable for Big Data applications.
I earned my B.S. in Computer Science from UCI in Spring 2012. Prior to this, I earned my A.A. (Mathematics) and A.S. (Computer Sience) from Fullerton College in Spring 2010
