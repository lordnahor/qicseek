Vgraph (1)

  • A Vgraph is a directed acyclic graph which models the versions and variants of a conceptual resource.

  • A Vgraph contains:

    • Resources (by reference), which are the nodes of the graph
    • Typed binary relationships, which are the arcs of the graph
      • Two relationships: is-derived-from, is-variant-of
