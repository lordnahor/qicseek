  http://www.ics.uci.edu/~ics1c/doc/assignment-3.html

If you check your individual score via the "ICS 1C Student Score
Request Form" (accessible as part of the "ICS 1C Student Information"
  http://www.ics.uci.edu/~ics1c/student.html
on the main ICS 1C Home Page
  http://www.ics.uci.edu/~ics1c/
), you'll note that there are 2 scores for Lab Exercise 3:
  H2 = Homework 2, First part of Lab Exercise 3. 
  H3 = Homework 3, Second part of Lab Exercise 3. 
The first part, H2, (a maximum of 3 points) included credit for
simply coming up with 5 questions.  Such credit was not duplicated
in evaluating the final submissions scoring on which part is
in the column headed "H3" (maximum score of 11.5 rounded up).
