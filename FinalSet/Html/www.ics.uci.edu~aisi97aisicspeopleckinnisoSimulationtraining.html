Director of Training/BAR Exam Tutorial

Michael Svoltowski

Michael Svoltowski has been the Director of Training for C.K.Kinnison and Associates for the past five(5) years. Michael developed the course and has spoken to over 10,000 students since he began this program independently in 2003. Michael wrote the BAR Exam tutorial and revises it annually. Michael has extensive training in motivational speaking and he also holds a teaching credential from Pace University in New York. Michael has taught our BAR Exam tutorial seminar all over the United States, including:


  • USBA's annual conference, New York, NY
  • Harvard University, 2005-current (annually)
  • Boston University, 2004-current (annually)
  • Stanford University, 2007-current (semi-annually)
