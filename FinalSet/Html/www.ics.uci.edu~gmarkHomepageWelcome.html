Gloria Mark

 
 

The world is continuing to shrink as social computing grows. Never before have people been able to connect to others on such a global and large-scale. This is a fascinating time to study how technology use and development are evolving. My research interest is in Computer Supported Cooperative Work (CSCW) which is the study of collaboration technologies and people.  I study multi-tasking in the workplace, distributed work and technology use, technology adoption, the impact of Web 2.0 technologies, and technology use in environmental crises. I invite you to take a closer look at some of the projects that my students and I are involved in.
