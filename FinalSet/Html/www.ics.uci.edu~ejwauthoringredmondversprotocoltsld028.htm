CHECKOUT (1)

  • The CHECKOUT method performs the following operations on the Vgraph associated with the Vportal at the Request-URI:

    • A new resource, known as the "working resource", is created at a location determined by the server. This resource is acted upon by authoring tools, accepting PUTs of intermediate and final results, and allowing properties to be read and set on it.
