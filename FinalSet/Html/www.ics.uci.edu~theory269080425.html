Finding Optimal Refueling Policies in Transportation Networks

authored by Shieu-Hong Lin

presented by Nate Gertsch

Abstract:

We study the combinational properties of optimal refueling policies, which specify the transportation paths and refueling operations along the paths to minimize the total transportation costs between vertices.
