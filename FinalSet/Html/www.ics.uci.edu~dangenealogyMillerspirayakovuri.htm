Spira Genealogy - The Yakov Uri Family


R' Yakov (Yentshi) Uri (1810- 1855), son of Pinchus Aryeh, ABD Turka, married twice. His first wife was Rifka Spira, daughter of R' Hersh Mylech Spira, and they had 4 known children. Rivka died in her father's lifetime. R' Yakov, remarried, to Rifka's niece Yitta Leah. She was a daughter of Sara Spira and Moshe Schonfeld. R' Yakov and Yitta Leah had additional children. This has led to some confusion as to which children were from his first wife and which from his second. This was further amplified because of the close family relationship between the wives.

  • Henya (18?? - ?) married Moshe Yosef Polimer. They lived in Galicia in a town pronounced in Yiddish: Pi-shetz-luv (Pshlov?) and had two children.
    • Hannah (18?? - 1???) married Elias Fisch, son of Peretz and descendent of the Vilna Gaon. They lived near Tarnov, Poland (Galicia), in a town called in Yiddish: V-yesh-ah-tzluv, and had three children.
      Esther Kreiswirth, Paul & Yitzchok
    • Napthali Hirsch (c.1840 - 1879) married Pearl Warfield and had five children.
      Annie Sarah, Rose Fischman, Samuel Kalman, Leon, & Adolph Pincus

  • Gittel Hinda (18?? - 1???) married R' Moshe Rottenberg, ABD Kozava, son of R' Alexander Rottenberg, ABD Kozava. They had one known son.
    • R' Yakov (18?? - 1937) ABD Kozava, married Yachna, daughter of Mordechai, and they had four children.
      girl, Miriam Sarah Rottenberg, Chana Fraidel Rottenberg & girl

      Ozer Babad

      Goldie Ostreicher, Chana, Shaindel, Matel, Gittel, Shlomo & Naftali

      Chaya Binah Beinhorn, Miriam Susha Rupp, Dovid, Rochel Schonfeld?, Shaindel Deutsch, Yitzchok Isaac, Yechezkel & Shlomo

  • Frieda (18?? - c.1900) married Shmuel Zanvil Kallech, son of Yeshaya Shlomo Zalman, and had three known children.
    • Yitzchok Kallech (18?? - 1933) married Itta Lehrer (1872 - 1950), daughter of Yehuda Lehrer, and had three boys. She left Istryk for Dresden, Germany at the start of the first world war, with the three boys. He remarried, and had two children with his second wife. From his first marriage a grandson and granddaughter Ernst and Ruth Lehrer are alive (Oct 2006). From his second wife, a grandson, Shmuel Kalech, lives now in Tel Aviv.

    • Yaakov Yantshi Kalich (18?? - 1???) had four known children.

    • Pinchus Aryeh Kallech (18?? - 1???)

  • Elyakim (c.1839 - c.1919) grew up in Galicia where he married his first wife with whom he raised a family. Unfortunately, his wife passed away at an early age, and he remarried Hinda (1851 - December 10, 1937), the daughter of R' Isaac Preiss from Kosice. They moved to Kosice and had three children. While there, he changed his surname from Uri to Uhr, which means emperor in Hungarian.
    • Leo (Yitzchok Yehuda) (1869 - 1950?) came to the USA aboard the SS Caronia on Feb 28, 1911, employed as waiter on the ship. He was already married. His final resting place is in Washington Cemetery, Brooklyn, NY.
    • Meir (18?? - 1???) has children in London, including Dr. Uhr.
      child

    • Avrohm (1879 - 1916) married Kreindel Mann (1878 - 1944), daughter of Akiva Mann and Esther Yilia Steinmatz, in 1900. They lived in Kosice, Slovakia and had ten children. Avrohom died in a trench during WW I. The majority of the family perished in the Holocaust.
      Sheba Rachel Fried, Yisrael Elimelech, Esther Fisher, Hena Malka Freid, Moshe, Eugene (Yankel Dov), Nuti Necha Deutch (Itzkovitz), Freidel Arlene Aranka Weiss, Chaim Zvi & Aron Yosef

      Shlomo, Meir, Tziporah, Sheva Rochel, Yehuda Leib, Moshe Yechiel & child
