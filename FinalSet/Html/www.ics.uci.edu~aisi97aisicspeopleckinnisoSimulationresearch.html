Director of Research

Katrina Robynsin

Katrina Robynsin is the Director of Research here at C.K.Kinnison and Associates. Katrina is an active member and Chairman on the Tribal Protection Legislation Committee,of the LLD&A, Law Legislation Discovery & Announcement; in which she proactively investigates and follows hot legislations of the day. While at Texas A&M University, Katrina headed a federally funded Research group on Native Americans, which won the Innovative Native American of the Year award in 2003. Katrina has headed Research projects at many acclaimed universities over the years, including:


  • UC San Diego, California
  • University of Washington, Washington
  • Texas A&M University, Texas(annually)
  • Stanford University,(semi-annually)

To find out about the latest legislations,please email Katrina.
