Bio:

Our friend Xiao Guai is one of the hard-workingest students we've ever encounter. He has been seen in the library, investigating the profoundest books about astronomy of gastronomy or about Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, that none of us understands. He was caught once reading <On the application of the quantum physics for analyzing the confucian philosophy >. He always has the hunger4knowledge look on his face, and is always ready to learn more.

Xiao Guai is also good listener. He always listens to our problems quietly. He doesn't say much but at least he doesn't argue. He is just a little shy.

我们的好朋友小怪

我们的好朋友小怪是个勤奋好学的孩子. 我们只在图书馆见到他, 每次都是眉头紧锁, 一副认真学习的表情. 他总是看一次些我们都不懂得文革书, 或者天体论的书. 有一次被我们抓到, 他居然在看<论量子物理在孔子哲学中的应用>, 让我们敬佩万分.
