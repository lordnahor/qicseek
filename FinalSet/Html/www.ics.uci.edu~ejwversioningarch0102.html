The meeting notes from the informal meeting of the Working Group on
Distributed Authoring on the WWW at AOL Productions, San Mateo, California,
on July 10, are now publically available at:

http://www.ics.uci.edu/~ejw/authoring/sanmateo/

This web site contains:
- Executive summary
- Final agenda
- Participants
- Detailed meeting notes
- Slides from presentations

This was a very lively and informative meeting with intelligent, informed
presentations and discussion on issues concerning distributed authoring on
the web.  If you're receiving this message, I suspect you'll find this site
of interest.
