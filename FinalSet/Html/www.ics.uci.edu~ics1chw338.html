  3. What is Lynx?
    Lynxis a browser that is used on the World Wide Web. It is a user friendly hypertext interface for users on unix and vms platforms. It can run gopher, HTTP, WAIS, FTP, or NNTP. Lynx is also a client program. Browsers are important for the user to "surf the Web".Other examples of browsers such as Lynx are Mosaic and Cello. We always use Mosaic, but why not Lynx?
