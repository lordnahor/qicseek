Hirschberg family

Jakob HIRSCHBERG (c1760 - Jan 12, 1819) landlord and moneylender, was the son of Hirsz HENDLOWICZ and Rachel, and married Rebeka (daughter of Nattan and sister of Rozel). He is listed as owning house #20 Judenstadt in 1797. He was elected as a Vorsteher (councilman) of Kazimierz in the elections of June 18, 1809 [see Balaban's Historja Zydow w Krakowie i na Kazimierzu 1304-1868 (The history of Jews in Krakow and the Kazimierz region, 1304-1868), volume 2, chapter 29, pp. 567-568].
