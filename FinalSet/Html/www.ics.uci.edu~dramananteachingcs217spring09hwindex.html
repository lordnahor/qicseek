Homework

Instructions

Late policy
No late homework will be accepted. Please hand it what you have by the due date/time so as not to get zero credit.

What to hand in
1. For math problems/derivations, show intermediate work.
2. For programming problems, hand in code as well as any plots/output illustrating its functionality.
3. You are responsible for making handwritten solutions legible and straightforward to follow.

You can hand in homework in one of two ways:

1. Hard-copy submission
Submit a hard-copy by the start of class on the due date.

or

2. Electronic submission
Submit an electronic copy into the appropriate homework folder in the EEE dropbox. If you are handing in more than one file, please zip or tar it into one directory whose name contains the homework assignment and your name - e.g., "Jane_Doe_HW1".
