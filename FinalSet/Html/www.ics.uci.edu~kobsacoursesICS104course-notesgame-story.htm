Storyline


Guidelines:
  • Player understands the story line as a single consistent vision (Desurvier 2004)
  • Every puzzle should relate to the story (Federoff 2002)
  • Provide consistency between the game elements and the overarching setting and story to suspend disbelief (Desurvier 2004)
  • Player discovers the story as part of game play (Desurvier 2004)
  • Player is interested in the story line.
    The story experience relates to their real life and grabs their interest (Desurvier 2004)
  • Player spends time thinking about possible story outcomes (Desurvier 2004)
  • Player is interested in the characters because they are like him/herself, and develop as action occurs (Desurvier 2004)
