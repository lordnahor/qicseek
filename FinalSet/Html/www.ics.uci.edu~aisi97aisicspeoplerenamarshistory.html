The hospital is based around American Indian culture. Organizations such as Tribal Natives have donated artifacts. All of the Native American tribes have a template placed hanging on the wall. Each one is a representitive of their tribe. For example, I am from the Cherokee tribe so their would be a template of the history of my tribe. Individualized templates are placed for recognition and to pay a respect to our ancestors.
