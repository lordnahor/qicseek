Schedule

All assigned readings are from the Perkovic text. It is a good idea to skim the assigned reading before the lecture for the main ideas, attend lecture, and then to go through the assigned reading again to fill in the details that you missed, both in your initial skim of the reading and in the lecture.

Several lectures have little or no reading corresponding to them. In some cases, this is because a block of reading corresponds to more than one lecture. In other cases, the material covered in that lecture is not discussed in the textbook.
