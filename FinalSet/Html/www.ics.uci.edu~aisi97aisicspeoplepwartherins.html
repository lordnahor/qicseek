Insurance Policy

To guarantee a satisfactory fishing experience, Phisherman Phil's offers a fishing insurance policy. This policy states that, when chartering a boat, the person chartering can pay an additional 15-25 percent, (depending on the season). This money is non-refundable. In the event that a charter catches no fish, the cost of the trip is refunded. Or if a predetermined requirement is not met, a portion may be refunded. The cost currently stands at 15%.
