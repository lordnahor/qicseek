Tucker Group



A mathematical sculpture by DeWitt Godfrey, unveiled at the Technical Museum of Slovenia as part of the Bled'07 excursion. It depicts a regular map of quadrilaterals, hexagons, and 16-gons on the double torus, the Cayley graph of the unique genus-two group as discovered by Thomas Tucker.
