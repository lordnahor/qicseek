Pisek family

Hirschel Kalman PISEK (c1753 - Jan 9, 1813) was the son of Kalman Lewkowicz (c1730 - 1795-98) and Feigel. (Kalman later remarried to Mindel.) Hirschel married Rachel and they had at least 6 children. He remarried to Dwoyre.
