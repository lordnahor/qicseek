Project 1: Mobile Learning through UCI
The UCI Distance Learning Center considers developing a mobile learning platform. The task of this project is
  1. to gather user expectations on mobile learning, from UCI undergraduate students to external learners of all ages,
  2. to look at what has been done at other places,
  3. to look at the suitability and customizability of the official mobile Moodle app and other mobile Moodle apps,
  4. to look into exploiting the new technical capability of smartphones,
  5. to make design recommendations and implement them in mockups/wireframes, and
  6. to test those mockups with users and possibly revise them
