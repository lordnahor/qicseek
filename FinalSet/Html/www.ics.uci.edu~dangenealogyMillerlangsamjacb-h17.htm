The Jacob Langsam Family


Some background email from the family can be found here.

Jacob Langsam was married to Rivkah and had one known son. They lived in Brooklyn at 1445 East 19th Street.

  • Phillip (Feivel) (1869 - 1945), a tailor, married Fanny, daughter of Morris and Yetta Grumet, in 1897. They had six known children. Phillip sailed from Hamburg, Germany aboard the Patricia and arrived in NY on July 4, 1887. He was originally from Jaroslaw, Poland. In 1919, when he naturalized, he and his family were living at 1461 St Marks Avenue, Brooklyn, NY.
    • Rose (Oct 9, 1897 - ?) became a teacher. She married late and had no children.
    • Jack (Oct 10, 1899 - ?) worked for his cousin, Paul Fischer.
    • Becky died at two years of age.
    • Sophie (Feb 11, 1904) married a grocery clerk.
    • Gertrude (Dec 3, 1907- 1984) married Alvin Udell (1906 - 1987), the son of a well-to-do dress manufacturer. They had one known son.
