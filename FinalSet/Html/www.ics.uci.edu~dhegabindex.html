I am currently a Master's Student in Informatics at the Donald Bren School of Information Sciences at UC Irvine focusing on human computer interaction (HCI), computer supported collaborative work (CSCW), ethnography, ubiquitous computing, and user design methods.



I am part of the Hana Lab and the LUCI Lab. I work with Judy Olson and Don Patterson for dissertation research on adult women's participation in technical learning organizations and informal maker groups. I am also an American Association of University Women (AAUW) Selected Professions Fellow, a scholarship recipient/finalist for Google's Anita Borg Scholarship Fund, and a recent recipient of UCI's Center of Organizational Research Graduate Student Fellowship.
