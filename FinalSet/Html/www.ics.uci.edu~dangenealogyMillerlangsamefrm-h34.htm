The Efraim Langsam Family of Lutowska, Poland


Efraim Langsam (1854 - 1929), son of Pesach, was married twice. His second wife was Chaya Sprecher of Lemberg and they had two known children. At first they lived in Lutowska but then moved to a farm. His wife perished in the Holocaust. He had sons, possibly from his first marriage, that settled in the USA before the Holocaust.

  • Sarah was married to a Steckel and had one known son.
    • Jack lives in Canada.
  • girl lived in Canada and did not marry.
  • Pesach (April 05, 1916 - September 28, 1995 [Tishrei 4]) was married to Regina/Rivka Chwerd and had four children. He changed his surname to Sprecher at the conclusion of World War II. Pesach had studied in the Bobowa yeshiva in Myslenice, Poland for a time and also lived in Tyrawa Woloska, Poland. He settled in the United States after the war.
