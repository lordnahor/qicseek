The Abraham Abba Langsam Family


Abraham Abba Langsam had one known son. This information comes from public records, as researched by Ms. Estelle Guzik and Ms. Suzan Wynne.

  • Leon (Yom Tov) (1864 - December 29, 1960) married Bertha/Beila (1872 - Oct 19, 1939) had five known children. Leon and Bertha came to the USA in 1888 and were naturalized. They are both buried at Mount Hebron Cemetery in Flushing NY (Ohave Shalom - Block 2). In 1900 they lived at 131 Pitt Street. If Leon was a doctor, lived at 848 Galber (?) Ave, Brooklyn NY in 1904, and had a brother Selig Langsam, the families are connected. Adolf (Selig's son) Langsam's ship manifest seems to imply there is a connection from the H59 section to the H19 section of the tree.
