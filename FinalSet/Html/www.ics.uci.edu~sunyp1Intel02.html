Intel Domestic Robot

 


 

Intel Research's Personal Robotics project aims to develop useful robotic assistants for indoor, populated environments. To transition robots from structured, assembly line scenarios to more natural, unstructured environments requires advances in perception, reasoning under uncertainty, and human-robot interaction.

 

 

I specifically worked on the domestic robot for senior citizen. My role was developing scenarios to demonstrate what robot can do and designing a touch-screen interface that allows elders to control a domestic robot that assists with aging in place.
