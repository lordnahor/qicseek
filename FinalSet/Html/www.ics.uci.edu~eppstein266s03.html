ICS 266, Spring 2003:
Computational Geometry

General Course Information

Coursework will consist of biweekly homeworks and a comprehensive final exam. Group work on homeworks is permitted; each student should turn in his or her own copy of the homeworks. The course text will be Computational Geometry Algorithms and Applications, 2nd ed., by de Berg, van Kreveld, Overmars, and Schwarzkopf (Springer-Verlag, 2000). The course meets Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays, 2:00 - 2:50 in CS 253.
