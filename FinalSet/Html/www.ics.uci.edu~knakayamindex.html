Brief Biography

    Kiyoshi Nakayama is currently a Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science at University of California, Irvine, where he initiated several projects with Professors Lubomir Bic, Michael Dillencourt, Jack Brouwer, Nalini Venkatasubramanian, and Eli Bozorgzadeh. He also collaborates with Smart Grid Research Group at California Institute of Technology and NEC Corporation. Prior to UC Irvine, he received his M.S. and B.S degree in Engineering from Soka University, where he was fortunately advised by Prof. Hitoshi Watanabe, the recipient of IEEE Gustav R. Kirchhoff Award in 2010.
