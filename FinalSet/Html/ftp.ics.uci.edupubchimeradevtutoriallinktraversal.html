Traversing the Link between the Images

  To traverse between the images, you can click with the left mouse button from within the anchor area of any of the images. This will cause the anchors in the other images to flash.

You may traverse to a closed image. To see this close wright_flight.jpg and earthrise.gif -- leave bill.gif open. Click in the anchor area in bill.gif. Both wright_flight.jpg and earthrise.gif will appear with their anchors flashing.
