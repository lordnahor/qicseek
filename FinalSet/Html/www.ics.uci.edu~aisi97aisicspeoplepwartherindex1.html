Phisherman Phil's
Charter Fishing

Phisherman Phil's Charter Fishing is an organization which allows a quality fishing experience for anyone, rich or poor. We have fishing trips to several locations in the California Channel Islands. There are either public trips, scheduled for weekdays, or private charters which occur on weekends.

Our fleet currently consists of two boats. One medium-sized sportfisher and one full-size party boat. Rod rentals are available for all trips.

For reservations or questions contact Phil Warther call at 642-0939, or email at: pwarther@ics.uci.edu, or go to Phil's home page to learn more about our founder, CEO, and captain of fishing operations.
