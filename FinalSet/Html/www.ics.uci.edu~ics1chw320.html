  • What is relationship between HTML and SGML?

     As found in James Powell's Introduction to HTML, HTML (Hypertext Markup Language)is just one application of SGML that is used to "mark-up" web documents. Both HTML and SGML were invented by Tim Berners-Lee of CERN. In fact, Hypertext Markup Language states that HTML is a generalized SGML language that can be used for a wide variety of applications.

  • What kind of person what Vincent van Gogh and what movement in modern art did he influence?

     Vincent van Gogh was an immense influence on the coming of modern art's post-expressionism, as can be noted in his work within Le WebLouvre, Paris. Vincent was also noted to be a great influence on Fauvism. In his personal life, Van Gogh was tormented by his lack of unrequitted love for both women and Christ, but he had a loving brother that funded his artistic pursuits. Through the course of his short ten years as an artist, Vincent managed to produce 800 paintings and an equal amount of sketches/renderings.
