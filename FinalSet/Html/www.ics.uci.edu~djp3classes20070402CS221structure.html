This is primarily a project and presentation based course.

The basic information covered in the class will be:

  • Markov Models
  • Latent Semantic Indexing
  • Factored Markov Models
  • Hidden Markov Models
  • Collaborative Filtering
  • Dynamic Bayesian Networks
  • Stochastic Reasoning on Markov Models
