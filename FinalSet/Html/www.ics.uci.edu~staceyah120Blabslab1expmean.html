Variance and Standard Deviation of the Sample Mean: Exponential Data

We know from Theorem 6.2.3 that the variance of the sample mean is the variance of the population divided by the sample size. The variance of an Exponential distribution with rate parameter 4 is 1/(42) = 0.0625. Thus, the variance of the sample mean for a sample size of 10,000 is 0.0625/10000 = 6.25 x 10-6, and the standard deviation is
