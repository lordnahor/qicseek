UCI OAC Open Access Labs

Based on information in UCI OAC EA Package

Among the services of the Office of Academic Computing are the labs available for student, staff and faculty use which are located on the first floor of the Engineering Gateway building. These labs are open 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

The X-terminals, located in Lab A (E1131) and shown as "X Facility" in the following map, are appropriate for use in ICS 1C.
