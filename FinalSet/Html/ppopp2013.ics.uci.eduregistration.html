Cancellations

Cancellations/requests for refunds must be in writing and received before February 9, 2013. Cancellations are subject to a $50 (325 CNY) processing fee. No cancellations after this date will be allowed, although another person may be substituted for a previously registered attendee assuming he/she fits into the same pricing category.
