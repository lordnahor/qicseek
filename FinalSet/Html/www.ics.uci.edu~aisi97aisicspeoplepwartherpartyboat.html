Boat Information
Party Boat

Our party boat is a 54 foot vessel, know as "Blue Moon", equipped to handle up to 50 people for fishing purposes. It has two 700 gallon bait tanks, one on each end. There are sleeping facilities below decks, designed to accomodate everyone on board. Our boat also features a full sized galley, and for the not quite as avid fisherman, there is even a television in the galley.

At our captain's disposal are the latest satellite navigation systems: the most advanced fish finding sonar and infared equipment and a direct radio line to air patrols in case they fishing hot spots.

Overall, "Blue Moon" is one of the most advanced fishing cruisers on the water. This coupled with the expertise of our captains will insure a great fishing experience for your money.
