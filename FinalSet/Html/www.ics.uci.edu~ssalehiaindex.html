I am a Computer Science PhD student at the University of California, Irvine, advised by Prof. Richard Lathrop. My research lies at the intersection of machine learning, and bioinformatics. I am interested in applying advanced computation to discover new cancer therapeutics.
I grew up in the northern part of Iran and received my undergraduate degree in Electrical Engineering from University of Tehran. I earned a Masters in Computer Science from the University of California, Irvine in 2011.
