Australian Embassy



Next door to the conference site. I had been hoping to take some photos of the building to illustrate its Wikipedia article, but under French law photos of buildings remain subject to the copyright of the architect and (at least under Wikimedia Commons consensus) embassarial extraterritoriality does not apply, so this will have to remain a personal snapshot.
