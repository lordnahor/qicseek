WELCOME


We here at CTNNCA want to extend a sincere "welcome". Located in the west wing of CTNN Medical Center is the CTNNCA. CTNNCA is a business association dedicated to the overall health and well-being of the tribal members and anyone that seeks our services. The Association was founded in July 1997 by Dr. Earlroy Pride M.F.C.C., and Dr. Miguel Hernandez M.F.C.C. to provide assistance and professional services to the Net Native community. As licensed professional counselors they saw a need to help their tribal members deal with life's stresses. Some life stresses include death, divorce, low or poor self esteem, family or relationship difficulties, anger, and depression. While only physicians can prescribe medication, counselors often work with pediatricians and psychiatrists when medication is needed.
