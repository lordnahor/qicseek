Company Beginnings

C.K.Kinnison and Associates was founded by Christina Kinnison,Attny. in 2012, and is currently owned and operated under Christina. We specialize in Tribal Law and more specifically Native American rights and protection thereof. Christina has ten years experience as a trial lawyer in federal law representing American Indians in many successfull cases. She has worked with the BIA,the IHS and more recently the local tribal government here in Orange County, CTNN, assisting Native Americans both locally and nationwide. She is currently a member of the NASL (National Association of Specialized Law), and is the Director of the Tribal Law and Protection Committee. With an experienced staff of 25, C.K.Kinnison and Assocites has the latest information on developing legislation and is home to one of the top rated training and tutorial courses for the BAR exam available to future lawyers.(U.S. News and World Report; Feb.2013) Obtain FREE legal advice through our CHAT! page from one our experienced attorneys, or check out our Frequently asked Questions?? page for further information.
