Outline of Projects

 

Your project will consist of learning about the topic and writing a report and presenting what you have learned. You are not expected to build or design anything.

Guidelines

  • Each group should consist of exactly 5 students (except one group may be of 4 as there are 109 students)
  • By week 3 (Friday April 19th) must have formed a group and selected a topic (see list of topics below)
  • All members of a group will get the same points on project (15 on report and 5 on presentation)
  • Each topic can be chosen (email to instructor) by at most two groups (first come first serve basis)
  • 5 page report due on Friday June 7th (submit softcopy to corresponding EEE dropbox) and should contain the following sections:
    • Abstract/Summary (1/3 page)
    • Introducing the Topic (2/3 page)
    • Challenges and Problems in that Topic (1 page)
    • New Ideas/Concepts/Protocols Proposed Recently (1 page)
    • Advantages of New Ideas/Concepts/Protocols Proposed (1 page)
    • Disadvantages and/or Problems to be Solved/Addressed (1 page)
  • Weeks 7, 8 and 9: 10min presentation for each group in class (we will take 80 min in last three lectures for presentations, slots assigned on first contact basis)

Topics

  • New Networking Architectures and Concepts:

    • Topic 1: Software Defined Networking (SDN) -- allocated to 2 teams
    • Topic 2: Content-centric Networking (CCN) or Named Data Networking (NDN) -- allocated to 2 teams
    • Topic 3: Active Networking -- allocated to 1 team

  • Content Delivery Networks:

    • Topic 4: Content Delivery Networks (CDN) -- allocated to 2 teams
    • Topic 5: Peer-to-Peer CDN -- allocated to 2 teams
    • Topic 6: Predictive (Preloading of) Mobile Content -- allocated to 2 teams

  • Wireless Networking:

    • Topic 7: New Wireless Technologies: e.g., Femto Cells and Personal Base Stations -- allocated to 2 teams
    • Topic 8: Cognitive Radios -- allocated to 2 teams
    • Topic 9: Delay Tolerant Networks (DTN) -- allocated to 1 team
    • Topic 10: Vehicular Networking (e.g., DSRC and VANETs) -- allocated to 2 teams

  • Internet Infrastructure and Data Centers

    • Topic 11: Anycast for Domain Name Service (DNS) -- allocated to 2 teams
    • Topic 12: Challenges in Data Center Networking -- allocated to 2 teams
