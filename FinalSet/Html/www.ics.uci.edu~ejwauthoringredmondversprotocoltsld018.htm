Vgraph (2)

  • Each version and variant of a conceptual resource is a separate resource, with a separate URI.

  • Nodes are referentially contained in a Vgraph

    • Nodes are identified by their URI

  • Nodes and arcs can have properties

    • Node properties in a Vgraph have a 1:1 correspondence to regular properties
