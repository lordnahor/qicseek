The infamous cake shot



While attempting to make this otherwise undistinguished photo, I unfortunately let the tripod with the camera on it tip and bash my camera, causing some cosmetic damage to the body but more significantly breaking its autoexposure function. So, I had to shoot the party on manual exposure and a lot of my guesses were pretty far from what they should have been... Camera has gone to the shop and I hope they can fix it.
