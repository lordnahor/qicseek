View from the train



Sitges (pronounced to rhyme with "stitches" -- it's Catalan for "silo") is a popular beach town near
Barcelona that Diana visited while I was giving my talk. She accidentally got onto the express train,
which passed by Sitges without stopping, so she had to get off and come back on a different train.
The added views of this detour easily made up for the slight delay.
