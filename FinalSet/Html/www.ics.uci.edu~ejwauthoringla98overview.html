IETF WEBDAV Working Group Meeting

Los Angeles, CA IETF
April 2, 1998

The WebDAV WG met Thursday, April 2, with approximately 45 people in attendance. Jim Whitehead began the meeting with a working group status report, including an announcement that the next (-08) revision of the WebDAV protocol specification will be sent to the IESG for Last Call. There will be an interim WebDAV meeting June 15-17, in Redmond, CA. Keith Swenson gave a brief overview of a standards effort he is starting on Web-based workflow. For the remainder of the meeting, Jim Davis led a discussion on the Advanced Collections Requirements draft, going through the requirements for external members and ordered collections requirement by requirement.
