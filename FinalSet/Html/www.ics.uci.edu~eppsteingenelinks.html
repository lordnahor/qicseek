Other network genealogy resources

There are many web pages and other network resources devoted to genealogy; I list here only a few large collections of pointers to other sites.
  • The Genealogy home page, Stephen A. Wood.
  • Macintosh genealogy software archive
  • Genealogy on the Mac
  • MyRoots software for viewing and editing GEDCOM files from Gene or other programs on a Palm Pilot.
  • Ancestry.com family history online.
  • FamilyReunion genealogy and family reunion resources and information.
  • GeneaNet genealogical database network. Automatically search a list of surnames, each with web or real-world addresses for further information.
  • Genealogy Online.
  • Genealogy Resources on the Internet, Paula Goblirsch, Minnesota.
  • Genealogy Resources on the Internet, Chris Gaunt and John Fuller.
  • Internet Genealogical Directory
  • RootsWeb site contains not only useful resource lists but also many volunteer genealogy projects.
  • Ancestors, 1996 PBS genealogy-related TV series
  • Journal of Online Genealogy
  • AOL Hispanic Genealogy Group
  • William Buckner has some tips on exchanging GEDCOM files with PC users.
