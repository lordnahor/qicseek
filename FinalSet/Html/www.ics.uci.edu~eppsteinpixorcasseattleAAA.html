AAA sign



This was our navigation landmark: our hotel was only a block away from this sign. We needed any help we could get finding the hotel the night we came in: after a couple minor mistakes we found ourselves trapped in a crowded parking lot a few blocks away and had to circle around several times to get where we wanted to be.
