CompSci 260 - Fundamentals of the Design and Analysis of Algorithms - Winter, 2013 (Dillencourt)

The homework problems, while highly recommended, are optional. Some of the problems are straightforward, others are a bit more challenging. They are intended to help you learn the material by applying it. This works if you make a serious attempt to solve them. If instead of trying to solve them yourself you prefer to ignore them, obtain solutions from a friend, or look them up on the internet, that is your choice.

Homework will not be collected or graded. Selected solutions will be discussed in the discussion sections. The assignments will be posted here.
