Tribal Council for Confederate Tribe of Net Natives

The Simulated Confederate Tribe of Net Natives has only two members of its Tribal Council, Omar Davis Tribal Chairman and Feather Martin Tribal Secretary. Together as a closely nit team we rigorously put forth effort to accomplish their objectives.

Often, we find ourselves seeking assistance from the consulting agencies within our Tribe. We hold meetings with businesses, schools, hospitals, and other business entities to communicate and exchange opinions on ways to make our community improve in all aspects.

While serving as Tribal Council our main objective is to communicate effectively with the Federal Government and promote our Native American ways. We are organizing a volunteer program of Native language speakers to visit our schools and hopefully encourage our youth to understand and speak the language. Other projects similar to this one are also being organized.
