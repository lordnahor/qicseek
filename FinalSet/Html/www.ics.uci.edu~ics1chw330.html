  4. Answer to Question #4

     One day I was taking a good hard look at the UC Irvine Office of Academic Computing home page when the Seminars and Workshops hypertext linked caught my attention. The OAC Friday Morning Seminars are free computer science related seminars that are offered to any UCI affilated person, including students. You can registar to participate though the Seminar Registration via Web form, this form is also made available at the Seminar and Workshop page. The currently announced seminar topics include seminars on the World Wide Web, X-Windows, and the UNIX operating system.
