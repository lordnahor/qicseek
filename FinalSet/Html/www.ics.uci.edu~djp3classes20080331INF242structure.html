  • This class will have weekly quizzes and/or writing to ensure engagement with the reading material.
  • Participation in discussions will be graded
  • Students will be required to lead discussions of papers.
  • There will be a programming/project for this course which will be either:
    • Write an application for the iPhone
    • Write an application for Google Android
    • Specifics of the application will be announced later.
    • Students will present their projects
  • There will be a final essay required of approximately 10 pages.

      • Desktop Computing
      • Ubiqutious Computing
      • Tangible Computing
      • Embodied Interaction
