Izenberg family

Mozes IZENBERG (c1780 - Jul 7, 1843), the son of Berl, was a cotton merchant and schoolteacher. He married Rachel HIRSCHBERG (1782-83 - Aug 20, 1843), the daughter of Jakob HIRSCHBERG. They lived in the house of Jakob and had at least 11 children.
