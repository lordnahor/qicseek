The Ephraim Langsam Family


Ephraim Langsam had one known son. This information comes from public records, as researched by Ms. Estelle Guzik and Ms. Suzan Wynne.

  • Eleazar (Louis) (1875 - 1933) came to the USA in 1888. He was married to Fanny (Feige Devorah), daughter of Lieb Bair, in the Bronx, in 1933. Fanny was about 59 when she passed away on January 13, 1939. She is buried at Mount Hebron cememtery in Flushing, NY (Ohave Sholom - Block 2). They had seven known children.
    • Edward
    • Joseph (1896 - ?)

    • Sidney
