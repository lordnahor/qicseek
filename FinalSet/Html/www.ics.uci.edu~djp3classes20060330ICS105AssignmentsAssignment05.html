  • Goals: The purpose of this assignment is:
    • to interpret your evaluation fair data
  • Instructions
    • This is a group project, submit information as a group
    • Turn in a report on paper to my office ICS 214 by Tuesday June 13th at 10am (this is the final exam slot for our class). Forget the electronic drop box.
    • The report should contain
      • Your original hand drawn proposals juxtaposed against the final website and mobile design (if applicable)
        • I would like an analysis of why there are differences between the two. That may include:
        • What differences are the result of technological limitations both in the fundamental technology and in your knowledge of how to implement it ?
        • What differences are the result of design choices made after the hand-drawn proposal?
        • What other reasons/ rationale caused a difference?
      • A presentation of the results collected in the evaluation fair
        • Each test that you performed coupled with the results of the test presented in a way that effectively communicates what you discovered. (graph, selected comments, table, etc.)
      • An interpretation of the results from the evaluation fair
        • Why did you get the results you did? Are they surprising? Are they useful?
        • Would you pick different evaluation metrics in the future? What would they be?
      • A proposed way forward
        • Given your results and your interpretation, what is an appropriate design response to the website that you have created?
        • How would you change the interaction patterns?
        • How would you change the functionality?
        • What usage patterns would you choose to abandon and what new patterns would you choose to support?
        • Would you add new pages to the interaction? Would you add new features? Which ones ?
      • A technology laundry list
        • What technologies, languages, libraries, techniques and software did you use to implement your design?
        • Where can one find more information on those technologies?
      • A URL
        • A url where your application can be found. Once the admin is finished, I am going to publish a list of the projects that were done in this class and hopefully get a small press release out. If you want to be linked into this endeavor, give me one website URL that you want me to publish your work under. If you have a mobile phone component you should add a link to that software on your website. You may also want to include a link to your C.V.
    • Depending on how you format your report, something that is 5 - 10 pages of double-space text plus additional pages for images is the target length. Competent analysis is more important than length.
