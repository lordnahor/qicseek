Fishing Locations

Catalina

Catalina is the most optimum fishing spot as far as variety compared to cost, compared distance from port. Catalina is good for perch, bonito, rockfish, bass, barracudas, and sharks. Party boat trip prices for the full day run about $37 or $40 with a pole rental. The three-quarter day Catalina trip goes for about $27, $30 with a pole rental.

San Clemente

San Clemente is offered only in day and a half trips, because of its distance from port, better than twice that of Catalina. You catch most of the same fish as catalina, with the addition of the more tropical fish, occasional yellowfin tuna, and yellowtail. These trips go to about $52 or $59 with rod rental.

14 Mile Bank

The 14 Mile bank is basically our one-half to three-quarter day trip. By far the most economical trip, the fishing isn't bad, roughly the same variety as Catalina, except you may get overcrowding problems. Priced at $19 or $25 with rod rental.
