The Distributed Systems Middleware (DSM) Group, under Professor Nalini Venkatasubramanian of the Department of Information & Computer Science at the University of California Irvine, is involved in various areas of research within the realm of distributed systems. 
  • Adaptive and Reflective Middleware
  • Middleware Support for Mobile Services
  • Quality aware Data Management
  • Distributed Multimedia and Applications
