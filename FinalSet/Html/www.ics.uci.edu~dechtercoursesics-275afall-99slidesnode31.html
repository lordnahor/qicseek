Further Informations

ECLiPSe has been developed at the European Computer-Industry Research Centre (ECRC) in Munich. It is being further developed and extended with new functionalities and results of ECRC's research. Although a result of research work, ECLiPSe is close to product quality. Requests for further information and orders should be sent to 

eclipse_request@ecrc.de
There is also an ECLiPSe user group mailing list.     Contributions to this list can be sent to 
eclipse_users@ecrc.de
requests for being added to or removed from this list to eclipse_request@ecrc.de. This manual, other reports and various public domain software can be obtained by anonymous ftp from   
ftp.ecrc.de (141.1.1.1)
in the directory /pub/eclipse.
