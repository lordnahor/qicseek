Homework 1

Due Jan. 15 at 11:55pm

 

Important note: The main point of the following tasks is to describe what exactly you did and what you experienced when navigating the website of  Task 1, and when working with the technical system you chose in Task 2. In cases of unfulfilled expectations, you should additionally explain what you intended to achieve, why you decided to perform a specific action to achieve this aim, and in what respect that action did not deliver what you expected.

 

Task 1. Visit the website of Pope John Paul II, find out which speeches he gave in January 1992 and list the first three of those speeches. Describe stepwise your actions and experiences when carrying out this task, at a level of detail that allows one to replicate your steps. It does not really matter whether you actually find the correct solution, but try your best.

Note: Only use the Firefox, Safari or Google Chrome web browsers for this task, and make sure Java/JavaScript is on. Give up after 10 minutes if you don't succeed (but still describe your experiences).

 

Task 2. Describe a usability problem that you encountered in the past. This is not restricted to software systems, but may relate to any man-made system. Check out http://www.baddesigns.com/examples.html to refresh your memory.
Explain the problems that you encountered and their impacts on you. Describe your suggestions for improvement. Do not write more than half a page, unless you need more space to describe the problem or your suggestions. Try to express your experiences in terms of the central measurable human factors for user interface evaluation that we learned in class.
